**Try Out SLua (Lua in Second Life) on the Beta Grid!**

Experience the next evolution of scripting in Second Life with SLua. Follow these steps to dive in:

## 1. Create an Account

1. Go to [join.secondlife.com](https://join.secondlife.com).
1. Sign up for your free Second Life account.

## 2. Install the Alpha Client

1. Visit [second.life/alternate-viewers](https://second.life/alternate-viewers).
1. Download and install **Second Life Project Lua Editor**.

## 3. (Optional) Connect to the Beta Grid

1. Launch **Project Lua Editor**.
1. Go to **Me > Preferences > Advanced** and enable **Show Grid Selection at login**.
1. At the login screen, select **Beta Test Grid (Aditi)** from the drop-down menu.
1. Click **Log In**.

This is now optional, as there are multiple regions on the main (default) grid of Second Life that can be used with the Lua alpha viewer. Simply open the World Map (Click the **Map** button, type **Control-M** or select **World > Map**) and type **Slua** in the Region Name field to locate an available testing region.

## 4. Teleport to an SLua Region

1. Click the **Map** button.
1. Search for **"SLua"**.
1. Select a region and click **Teleport** to enter a sandbox area.

## 5. Start Scripting in SLua

1. Enter Build mode (**Build > Build**).
1. Rez a box, then open its **Content** tab.
1. Click **New Script** and start coding in **SLua**!

For more information about scripting with SLua, visit [SLua Alpha](https://wiki.secondlife.com/wiki/SLua_Alpha).
